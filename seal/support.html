How do I add a friend?

In person. Open the Circle tab, have your friend scan your QR code, then they tap their security key (or use their passkey) on your phone. Check that the emoji phrase matches on both screens — that's the proof the connection is real.

What's the difference between the silver and brass rings?

Silver = passkey identity (Face ID). Brass = Verified identity, rooted in a physical FIDO2 hardware security key like a YubiKey.

I lost my security key. Can I recover my account?

If you registered a backup key, endorse a new device with it. If you lose all registered keys, the identity is unrecoverable — by design. There is no email or SMS reset, because those are exactly how accounts get stolen.

Why does Seal need a hardware key or passkey?

It's what makes everyone in Seal provably human and provably someone you've met. Bots can make accounts; they can't stand in a room with you.

My security key isn't being read over NFC

Hold the key flat against the top edge of the back of your iPhone and keep it still. If your key has a PIN and NFC keeps failing, try USB-C instead.
