Mockingbird

Say it right.

A pronunciation coach that hears your real voice and shows you exactly what to fix — from a child's first English words to fine-tuning an accent.

Download on the App Store

What it does

Hear yourself

Say a word or sound and Mockingbird scores how close you are, measuring the real acoustics of your voice.

See your voice

A live vowel map shows your pronunciation moving toward the target in real time.

For kids & beginners

Picture words, big stars, and a cheering bird — almost no reading needed, in any first language.

15 sounds

Every English vowel and diphthong, each with how-to tips and example words you can hear.

Daily & streaks

Five sounds a day, a streak to protect, a mastery map, and achievements to collect.

Private by design

Core analysis runs on your device. Kids' mode never sends audio anywhere.

Made for everyone learning to speak

Whether you're a child learning your first words, an English learner softening an accent, or anyone who wants clearer speech — Mockingbird turns “say it again” into something you'll actually come back to.
