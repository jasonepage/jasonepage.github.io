Mockingbird — Support

How can we help?

Mockingbird is a pronunciation coach. Here are answers to the most common questions. If you need anything else, email jasonepage@gmail.com and you'll get a reply.

Getting started

Allow microphone access when prompted, pick a word or sound, tap the microphone, and say it. You'll get a score (or stars in the picture-word mode) with a tip on what to adjust.

The app needs my microphone — why?

Mockingbird analyzes the sound of your voice to score your pronunciation. The microphone is used only while you're practicing. If you declined the permission, you can re-enable it in the iOS Settings app under Mockingbird.

My scores seem low

Try “Calibrate to your voice” in Settings (say “eee” and “ahh” once) so scoring matches your voice. For sharper, native-level feedback you can also turn on the optional AI coach.

What is the optional AI coach?

It's an optional feature that gives more precise feedback by sending your recording to Google's Gemini service to be scored. It's off by default and requires you to add your own free API key in Settings. The app works fully without it — all core scoring runs on your device.

Is my data private?

Yes. Core analysis runs on your device, and the kids' picture-word mode never sends audio anywhere. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Is it good for children?

Yes — the picture-word mode is designed for young learners and works in any first language using pictures, audio, and stars. Children's audio is analyzed entirely on the device.
