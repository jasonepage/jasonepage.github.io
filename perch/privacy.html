Perch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Perch is built to collect as little as possible. The homes you save, along with your notes, photos, tags, and scores, are stored on your device and synced only through your own iCloud account. There are no Perch accounts and no login.

Saving a listing

When you save a home — by pasting a link, sharing from Safari, or snapping a photo — Perch fetches that page or reads the image on your device to build a clean listing card. Perch requests only the specific page you choose to save. It does not crawl the web or bypass paywalls.

Optional cloud tracking (Perch+)

Perch+ is an optional subscription that can watch the homes you've saved for price and status changes and alert you. Cloud tracking is off until you turn it on. When enabled, only the listing links you choose to track and your saved-search criteria are sent to Perch's service so it can re-check those public pages and notify you. Your notes, photos, ratings, and shortlist never leave your device. The service stores only the minimum public data needed — a listing URL and its last-seen price and status — and nothing that identifies you.

Purchases

If you subscribe to Perch+, the subscription is handled entirely by Apple. Perch receives only an Apple-signed receipt confirming it's active — we never see your payment details, and there is no separate Perch account tied to your purchase.

What we don't do

  • No accounts. Your identity is your Apple ID / iCloud.
  • No ads, no trackers, no selling data — ever.
  • We never sell you as a lead to real-estate agents.
  • No analytics that contain personal information or your saved homes.
  • No server-side copy of your library.

Network activity

Perch's network requests are: fetching the listing page or image you explicitly choose to save; Apple services (iCloud sync and the App Store); and, only if you enable Perch+ cloud tracking, sending the listing links you track to Perch's service as described above.

Your data, your control

You can export your entire collection to a portable file at any time, and you can erase all Perch data from your device (and your iCloud) from Settings.
