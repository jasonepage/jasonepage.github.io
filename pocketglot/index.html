Pocketglot — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Pocketglot is built to be private by design. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

  • Your conversations, progress, streaks, and achievements are stored only on your device.
  • Understanding what you type uses Apple's on-device Natural Language models. Nothing you type is sent to us or any third party.
  • The AI characters run entirely on-device using Apple Intelligence. Your messages are processed locally and never leave your phone.
  • Translations use Apple's on-device Translation feature. A language pack downloads from Apple once, then works offline; the content of your conversations is never sent to us.
  • Daily reminders, if you turn them on, are scheduled locally on your device.
  • There are no accounts, no logins, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.
  • We have no servers and therefore never receive your data.

If you delete the app, all of its data is removed with it. Because Pocketglot collects nothing, there is no data to request, export, or delete from us.
