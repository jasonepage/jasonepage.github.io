Quick answers

How do I play? Pick a scene — a café, a market, a taxi — and chat with the character by typing in Spanish. As you say the right things, your objectives at the top tick off. There's no single correct wording; it understands what you mean.

Stuck? Tap the translate icon on the character's lines to see what they said, or tap the lightbulb for a model phrase to try.

Do I need Apple Intelligence? The AI characters need a supported iPhone with Apple Intelligence turned on. Objective tracking and translation work on supported devices too.

Does it work offline? Yes — the scenes, scoring, and characters all run on your device with no internet. Translation needs a one-time language-pack download from Apple, then works offline.

Is it free? Yes — Pocketglot is completely free.

Privacy: Pocketglot collects nothing — all data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
