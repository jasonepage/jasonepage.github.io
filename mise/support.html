Mise — Support

We usually reply within a couple of days.

Need help, found a bug, or have a feature idea? Email jasonepage@gmail.com and we'll get back to you.

How do I add a recipe?

Tap Add Recipe and paste a recipe link, paste from your clipboard, take or choose a photo of a recipe, or scan a Mise card.

Can I plan meals and make a grocery list?

Yes. The Plan tab lets you add recipes to each day of the week, and Mise builds a single, aisle-sorted grocery list from everything you've planned.

The imported recipe looks off — can I fix it?

Yes. When Mise isn't fully confident (especially for photo scans), it opens a Review screen so you can correct the title, ingredients, steps, time, and servings before saving.

Where are my recipes stored?

On your device, and synced through your own iCloud account. There is no Mise account and no Mise server. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I back up or move my recipes?

Go to Settings → Export to save your entire library as a portable JSON file.

Does Mise work offline?

Yes. Your saved library is fully available offline. A network connection is only needed to import a recipe from a link.

Is Mise really free with no ads?

Yes — no ads, no trackers, no data selling.
