Five-Minute Overnight Oats

Stir tonight, eat tomorrow — the easiest breakfast you'll ever make.

5 min · Serves 1 · Mise Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 cup berries

Directions

  1. Stir the oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, and honey together in a jar.
  2. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Top with berries before eating.
