Sheet-Pan Lemon Herb Chicken

Crispy chicken and potatoes on one pan — minimal cleanup, maximum flavor.

45 min · Serves 4 · Mise Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1 lb baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Heat the oven to 425F.
  2. Toss the potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper on a sheet pan and roast for 10 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken, lemon slices, garlic, and oregano, and roast until the chicken is cooked through, about 25 minutes.
  4. Rest for 5 minutes before serving.
