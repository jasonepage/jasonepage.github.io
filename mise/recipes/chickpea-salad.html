Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

A no-cook, protein-packed lunch that gets better as it sits.

15 min · Serves 4 · Mise Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Combine the chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and feta in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk the olive oil, lemon juice, and oregano together.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad, season, and toss.
  4. Chill for 15 minutes before serving.
