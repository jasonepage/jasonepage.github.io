Honey Garlic Salmon Bowls

Sticky-sweet salmon over rice — a fast dinner that tastes like takeout.

25 min · Serves 4 · Mise Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Whisk the honey, soy sauce, garlic, and vinegar together.
  2. Sear the salmon in a hot pan, skin-side down, for 4 minutes, then flip.
  3. Pour in the sauce and spoon it over the salmon until glazed, about 3 minutes.
  4. Serve over rice and top with green onions and sesame seeds.
