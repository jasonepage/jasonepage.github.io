Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Crisp edges, soft centers, and pools of melted chocolate.

26 min · Serves 24 · Mise Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Heat the oven to 375F and line two baking sheets.
  2. Cream the butter and sugars until fluffy, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix in the flour, baking soda, and salt, then fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop onto the sheets and bake for 11 minutes, until the edges are golden.
  5. Cool on the pan for 5 minutes before transferring.
