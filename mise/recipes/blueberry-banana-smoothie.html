Blueberry Banana Smoothie

A bright, creamy three-minute blend to start the day.

3 min · Serves 2 · Mise Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Add the banana, blueberries, milk, yogurt, honey, and vanilla to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, about 45 seconds.
  3. Pour into two glasses and serve cold.
