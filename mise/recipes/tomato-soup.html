Classic Tomato Soup

Silky, comforting, and ready in half an hour — soup-and-grilled-cheese weather.

30 min · Serves 4 · Mise Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Melt the butter in a pot over medium heat and cook the onion until soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, broth, and sugar, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Blend until smooth, stir in the cream, season, and serve.
