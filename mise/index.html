Mise

A local-first cookbook.

Paste any recipe link and get a clean recipe in seconds — no ads, no clutter, no life story.

Why Mise

  • No ads. No pop-ups. No life story. Just the recipe, beautifully laid out.
  • Private by design. Recipes are extracted and stored on your device, and synced only through your own iCloud. There is no Mise server.
  • Yours forever. Export your whole library to a portable JSON file anytime. No account required.

What it does

  • Pull a clean recipe from a link, a shared card, a pasted photo, or a screenshot (on-device text recognition).
  • Search, sort, tag, and organize an offline library.
  • Cook Mode with step-by-step view, timers, and ingredient check-off.
  • Scale servings and switch between US and metric units.
  • Share a recipe as a beautiful card others can scan back into Mise.
