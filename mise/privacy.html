Mise — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Mise is built so there is almost nothing to collect. Your recipes are extracted and stored on your device, and synced only through your own iCloud account. We do not run servers, and we never receive, store, or process your recipes.

What we don't do

  • No accounts. Your identity is your Apple ID / iCloud.
  • No Mise servers. There is no backend that could see your data.
  • No ads, no trackers, no selling data — ever.
  • No analytics that contain personal information or recipe content.

Network activity

The only network requests Mise makes are: fetching the recipe page or image you explicitly choose to import, optional public metadata lookups, and Apple services (iCloud sync and the App Store). Mise does not crawl the web or bypass paywalls.

Your data, your control

You can export your entire library to a portable JSON file at any time, and you can erase all Mise data from your device (and your iCloud) from Settings.
