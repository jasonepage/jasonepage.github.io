Pawl — Support

How can we help?

Pawl is an iPhone commitment device for quitting gambling. Here are answers to common questions. For anything else, email jasonepage@gmail.com and you'll get a reply.

Getting started

Open the app and follow the short setup: grant Screen Time access, choose the gambling apps and websites you want blocked, pair your physical security key, and activate. Pawl then shields those apps and sites and shows a "Locked by Pawl" screen in their place.

What kind of security key do I need?

Pawl uses a standard USB-C FIDO2 security key (the same kind used for two-factor login), paired through Apple's built-in WebAuthn support. Once it's paired, put the key somewhere out of reach — that physical distance is the whole point. Lifting the block requires the key plus a 15-minute cooling-off timer.

I lost my security key / I'm locked out

If you pair a key and then lose access to it, you can still remove the block by deleting the app or turning off Screen Time for Pawl in iOS Settings — Pawl is designed as friction, not an inescapable lock, so you can never be permanently trapped. Your clean-day streak is backed up to your iCloud and will return if you reinstall. For help re-pairing a new key, email support.

Does the block really hold if I force-quit Pawl?

Yes. The shield is applied through Apple's Screen Time system and is enforced by the operating system, not by Pawl running in the background, so it stays in place even if the app is closed or your phone restarts.

What is a sponsor?

A sponsor is an optional trusted person you can link to your account. They approve your unlock requests and are alerted if your protection drops, so someone who cares is in the loop. Sponsors are entirely optional — Pawl works solo.

Why does Pawl ask me to sign in?

Signing in with your Apple ID is only needed if you want to use a sponsor or sync across devices. It lets Pawl connect you and your sponsor securely. If you're using Pawl solo, your streak and settings work through your own iCloud without a separate account.

Is my data private?

Pawl never sees which specific apps or websites you use — Apple's Screen Time only hands the app anonymous tokens. We collect the minimum needed to make sponsors and sync work, and nothing is sold or shared. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I cancel or remove everything?

Delete the app to remove the block from your device. If you created an account, email jasonepage@gmail.com to have your account and any associated data deleted.
