Pawl — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Pawl is built to protect your privacy. We collect as little as possible, we never sell or share your data, and the app is designed so that we can never see which specific apps or websites you choose to block. This policy explains what is and isn't collected.

What Pawl never sees

Pawl blocks gambling apps and sites using Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls) framework. By Apple's design, this framework gives the app only opaque, anonymous tokens — never the names of the apps or websites you select. Your specific choices stay private on your device; we have no way to read them.

Stored on your device and your iCloud

Your clean-day streak, relapse log, friction settings, and block configuration are stored on your device and may sync through your own private iCloud account if iCloud is enabled. This data is yours. It is removed from the device if you delete the app, and you can manage iCloud data through Apple's iOS settings.

Account data (only if you sign in)

If you choose to use a sponsor or sync across devices, Pawl asks you to sign in with Sign in with Apple. In that case we store, on a secure backend (Supabase), the minimum needed to provide those features:

  • An account identifier from Sign in with Apple (we do not receive your password, and we receive your email only if you choose to share it).
  • Sponsor links — the connection between a person quitting and their chosen sponsor.
  • Unlock requests and protection (tamper / heartbeat) status, so a sponsor can be notified.
  • A push-notification device token, used only to deliver those alerts.

If you use Pawl solo and never sign in, no account is created and none of the above is collected.

Push notifications

If you enable a sponsor relationship, Pawl uses Apple Push Notification service to send alerts (for example, an unlock request or a tamper alert). The device token used for this is tied only to delivering Pawl's notifications.

What we don't do

Pawl has no advertising, no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and no data brokering. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

Data retention and deletion

On-device and iCloud data is removed when you delete the app and clear iCloud data. If you created an account, you can request deletion of your account and all associated backend data by emailing natepage67@gmail.com, and it will be deleted.

Children

Pawl is intended for adults working to quit gambling and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email natepage67@gmail.com.
