Stargazer: Roll & Write ✦

A cozy, fully offline constellation puzzle for iPhone and iPad.

Place dealt star-shapes onto your sky chart, connect stars into constellations, and score against each night's goals. Collect named Star Signs in your Scrapbook, chase a fresh Daily sky, and unwind. No ads, no accounts, no tracking — just you and the night.
