Stargazer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Stargazer is built to respect your privacy completely. The app does not collect any data. It works fully offline, has no account, and contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

What we collect

Nothing. Stargazer does not gather, transmit, or sell any personal information, and no data ever leaves your device.

What's stored on your device

Your game progress, stats, streak, Scrapbook, and settings are saved locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. This information stays on your device and is removed when you delete the app. There is no online account and no cloud sync.

Notifications

If you turn on the optional daily reminder, that notification is scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to us or to any server.

Purchases

Stargazer is a one-time paid app with no in-app purchases. Any purchase is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store under Apple's privacy terms; we never receive your payment information.

No third parties

Stargazer contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. We don't share data with anyone, because we don't collect any.

Children

Stargazer is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the "Last updated" date above will change.
