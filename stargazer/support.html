Stargazer: Roll & Write — Support

How can we help?

Stargazer is a cozy, fully offline constellation puzzle. Here are answers to common questions. For anything else, email jasonepage@gmail.com and you'll get a reply.

How do I play?

Each round deals you three small star-shapes. Pick one from the tray, tap a star to preview it, rotate it to fit, then place it to connect stars into constellations. Score against the night's two objectives, plus a bonus for each named Star Sign you draw. A full night is seven rounds.

What's the difference between Daily and Free Play?

The Daily Puzzle is the same sky for everyone on a given day, with a streak and a shareable result. It resets at midnight, local time. Free Play is endless, relaxed nights you can start anytime.

What are Star Signs and the Scrapbook?

Star Signs are 19 named constellations. Drawing one earns bonus points and collects it permanently in your Scrapbook, which tracks how many times you've found each one and when you first did. Find all 19 to unlock a secret theme.

What are the planets and dashed rings on the board?

Those are bonus spaces. Draw all four lines around a planet to "orbit" it for bonus points, or draw the requested Star Sign over a sign slot (the dashed ring) for an extra bonus.

Is it really offline? Do I need an account?

Yes — Stargazer is 100% offline and needs no account or internet connection. There are no ads, no tracking, and no in-app purchases.

Where is my progress saved?

Your stats, streak, Scrapbook, and settings are stored on your device. There's no online account or cloud sync, so they live on that device and are removed if you delete the app.

How do I change sounds, haptics, theme, or motion?

Open Settings (the gear, top-right) to toggle Sound and Haptics independently and to pick a theme. Stargazer also respects iOS Reduce Motion, and supports VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, and a color-blind-safe board.

How do I turn on the daily reminder?

In Settings, enable "Daily reminder" and choose a time. It only works if you grant notification permission, and you can turn it off anytime.
