Hunch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Hunch is built to be private by design. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

  • Your guesses, questions, stats, streaks, and coins are stored only on your device.
  • Semantic closeness scoring uses Apple's on-device Natural Language models. Nothing you type is sent to us or any third party.
  • The "Ask the Keeper" feature runs entirely on-device using Apple Intelligence. Your questions are processed locally and never leave your phone.
  • There are no accounts, no logins, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.
  • In-app purchases (coin packs) are handled by Apple. We never see your payment information.
  • We have no servers and therefore never receive your data.

If you delete the app, all of its data is removed with it. Because Hunch collects nothing, there is no data to request, export, or delete from us.
