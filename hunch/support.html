Quick answers

How do I play? There's one secret word each day. Type any word and Hunch shows how close it is in meaning — cold (blue) to hot (red). Keep guessing to home in.

What is "Ask the Keeper"? An on-device AI that answers yes/no questions about the secret word without revealing it. It needs a supported iPhone with Apple Intelligence turned on; the rest of the game works on any device.

Does it work offline? Yes — the daily puzzle, scoring, and hints all run on your device with no internet. Only buying coins requires a connection.

What are coins for? Optional extra hints and questions. The daily puzzle is always fully playable for free.

Privacy: Hunch collects nothing — all data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
