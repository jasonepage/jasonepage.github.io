Illahee — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Illahee is built to be private by design. The app has no accounts, no logins, no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking, and we operate no servers that receive your data.

What we collect

Nothing is collected by us. We have no servers and receive no personal data. Because Illahee collects nothing on our end, there is no personal data for us to sell, share, request, export, or delete.

Your progress stays with you

Your learner profiles, lesson progress, streaks, and settings are stored on your device, and may sync through your own private iCloud account if iCloud is enabled on your device. This data is yours; it is removed from the device if you delete the app, and you can manage iCloud data through Apple's iOS settings.

On-device intelligence

Illahee uses Apple's on-device intelligence for its English-language coaching and for simple picture illustrations of word meanings. This processing runs entirely on your device and is not sent to us or to any third party. The app never sends Chinuk Wawa, or any audio of your voice, to a cloud service.

Microphone & camera

The microphone is used only when you actively record yourself for "say-it" and sounds practice, so you can play your own attempt back. The camera is used only while you are actively using the "name the world" feature, where on-device vision recognizes objects. Recordings and camera frames are processed on the device for that moment and are not collected, uploaded, or stored by us.

Children

Illahee is designed to be safe for young learners. Learner profiles hold no accounts, photos, or sensitive personal information, and all processing happens on the device. Illahee does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Learning content

Chinuk Wawa content shown in the app comes from verified material approved by the Grand Ronde Chinuk Wawa Language Program. The language is owned by the community, and the app follows Indigenous data-sovereignty principles (CARE) regarding how that content is used.
