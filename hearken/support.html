Hearken — Support

How can we help?

Hearken is a private app for recording an elder's spoken stories and turning them into a readable narrative and searchable records, entirely on the device. Here are answers to common questions. For anything else, email jasonepage@gmail.com and you'll get a reply.

Getting started

Open the app and tap Record to start a story, or choose the guided interview to be offered one gentle question at a time. Speak for as long as you like. When you stop, Hearken transcribes the recording on the device and helps turn it into a narrative plus a set of records you can review and correct.

Does the app make things up?

No — by design it cannot assert anything. Everything Hearken produces is an editable draft drawn only from what was actually said, for the speaker to confirm, change, or delete. Suggested records that aren't supported by the recording are filtered out, and the app flags uncertainty rather than guessing. The story stays the speaker's.

Why does it ask for the microphone, camera, or photos?

The microphone is used only while you are recording a story. The photo library and camera are used only when you choose to add a photograph to a story and describe it. Everything is processed on your device, and nothing is uploaded. You can change these permissions any time in the iOS Settings app under Hearken.

Does it work offline? Is my data private?

Yes. Hearken runs entirely on-device and works in airplane mode. There are no accounts, ads, tracking, or servers, and nothing leaves the phone unless you deliberately export a story. Before any export, the app records consent, and anything marked Restricted is removed from every export. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Keeping something private

You can mark any record, sentence, photograph, or an entire story as Restricted. Restricted content is excluded from every exported file — this is enforced when the export is written, not just hidden in the app. You can also delete a recording to save space while keeping its written story, or delete a story and all its data permanently.

For Grand Ronde Cultural Resources / Lifeways

Hearken is built to be a tool for the community, under tribal control. The interview questions and the archival "museum package" fields in the app are neutral placeholders pending your review. If you'd like to talk about the question bank, the archival schema, or data-sovereignty (CARE / OCAP) considerations, please reach out directly.
