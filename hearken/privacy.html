Hearken — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Hearken is built to be private by design. The app has no accounts, no logins, no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking, and we operate no servers that receive your data. The app is designed to work fully offline.

What we collect

Nothing is collected by us. We have no servers and receive no personal data. Because Hearken collects nothing on our end, there is no personal data for us to sell, share, request, export, or delete.

Your stories stay with you

Recordings, photographs, transcripts, narratives, records, and settings are stored on your device, protected by iOS data protection (encryption at rest). This data is yours. It is removed from the device if you delete the app, and within the app you can delete a recording while keeping its written story, or delete a story and all of its data permanently.

On-device intelligence & transcription

Hearken transcribes recordings and refines them into a narrative and structured records using on-device speech recognition and Apple's on-device intelligence. This processing runs entirely on your device and is not sent to us or to any third party. No recording, photograph, transcript, or derived text is uploaded to a cloud service by the app.

Microphone, camera & photos

The microphone is used only while you are actively recording a story. The photo library and camera are used only when you choose to add a photograph to a story and describe it. Recordings, photographs, and camera frames are processed on the device and are not collected, uploaded, or stored by us.

Sharing is always your choice

Nothing leaves your device unless you deliberately export a story. Before any export, the app records your consent, and any content you mark as Restricted — a record, a sentence, a photograph, or an entire story — is excluded from every exported file. When and how an exported file is shared afterward is entirely up to you.

Ownership & cultural governance

The speaker — and, at the speaker's choosing, the Confederated Tribes of Grand Ronde — owns the content. Hearken claims no rights to it. The app is built for cultural preservation under tribal control and is designed to be compatible with Indigenous data-sovereignty principles (CARE / OCAP). The interview questions and archival export fields in the app are placeholders pending review by Grand Ronde Cultural Resources / Lifeways staff.

Children

Hearken does not require accounts and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All processing happens on the device.
