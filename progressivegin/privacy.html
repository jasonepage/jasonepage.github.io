Progressive Gin — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Progressive Gin is a single-player card game that runs entirely on your device. It collects no data about you. There are no accounts, no servers, and no tracking.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not gather, store, or transmit any personal information, usage analytics, or device identifiers. We have no way to know who you are or how you play.

Offline by design

The game is fully offline and needs no internet connection. Your game progress and settings stay on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

No ads, no purchases, no third parties

Progressive Gin contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no third-party SDKs or analytics services. No data is shared with anyone because no data is collected.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data of any kind, it collects no data from children. It is safe for players of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.
